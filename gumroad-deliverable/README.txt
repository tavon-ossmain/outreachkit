Thanks for purchasing OutreachKit — the complete cold outreach system for AI agency founders.

HOW TO USE:
1. Open OutreachKit.html in any web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
2. Fill in your details: name, AI service type, and target industry
3. Click "Generate My Outreach System"
4. Copy-paste the emails, DMs, proposal, pricing, and objection handlers

That's it. Everything is customized to your specific AI service and industry.

WHAT'S INCLUDED:
- 5-email cold outreach sequence
- 3 LinkedIn DM scripts
- Professional client proposal template
- 3-tier pricing framework with recommended rates
- 5 objection handlers with word-for-word responses

TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS:
- Personalize the {{firstName}} and {{company}} placeholders before sending
- Send Email 1 on Monday or Tuesday mornings for best open rates
- Wait 2-3 days between each follow-up email
- Use the LinkedIn DMs for warm leads, emails for cold outreach
- Practice the objection handlers out loud before your first sales call
